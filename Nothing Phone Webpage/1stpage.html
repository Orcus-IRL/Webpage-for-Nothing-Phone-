About

Nothing Technology Limited (stylized as NOTHING) is a pioneering consumer electronics company based in the vibrant heart of London, England. Founded by the visionary entrepreneur Carl Pei, renowned for co-founding the trailblazing smartphone maker OnePlus, NOTHING is set to redefine the landscape of technology.

Backed by a stellar lineup of investors including iconic figures such as iPod inventor Tony Fadell, Twitch co-founder Kevin Lin, Reddit CEO Steve Huffman, and creative powerhouse YouTuber Casey Neistat, NOTHING is fueled by innovation and a commitment to pushing boundaries.

On February 25th, 2021, NOTHING announced an exciting partnership with Teenage Engineering, a collaboration aimed at infusing the brand's ethos with cutting-edge design aesthetics and groundbreaking products. The culmination of this partnership resulted in NOTHING's inaugural product, "ear (1)", which debuted on July 27th, 2021, setting a new standard for wireless audio innovation.

History

In 2020, Carl Pei, the co-founder of OnePlus, was working with the company alongside Pete Lau. Pei announced his resignation on 16th October 2020, so that he could start a new venture.Pei later raised up to $7 million from multiple investors to start up his venture including iPod inventor Fadell , Twitch co-founder Kevin Lin, Reddit CEO Steve Huffman, and YouTuber Casey Neistat. Pei announced the company, Nothing, on 27 January 2021. On 15th February 2021, Nothing acquired the Essential Products trademarks and brand nearly a year after the company shut down operations. On 25th February 2021, the company announced its first founding partner, Teenage Engineering, to produce the design aesthetic of the brand and its products.Nothing announced its first product on 27th July 2021, named the "ear (1)", which are wireless headphones. On 13th October 2021, the company raised up to $50 million and also announced a partnership with Qualcomm. On 9th March 2022, the same day that Nothing secured Series B financing, the company announced that it would hold a press conference on 23th March.During that event, the company announced its first smartphone, the "phone (1)".On 10th December 2022, Nothing opened its first physical store in London's Soho district.
